Yuanqiang Zhou - Biography

Yuanqiang Zhou was born in Xiangyang, China. He received the B.S. degree in mathematics and applied mathematics at the School of Science in 2013 and the M.S. degree in control science and engineering at the School of Astronautics in 2015, from the Harbin Institute of Technology, Harbin, China. Afterward, he studied at the Department of Automation, School of Electronic Information and Electrical Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Shanghai, China, where he received the Ph.D. degree in control science and engineering in 2020. From 2017 to 2019, he was a visiting scholar with the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Tandon School of Engineering, New York University, NY, USA.

Since October 2020, he has been working as an Associate Research Fellow with the Department of Chemical and Biological Engineering, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, Hong Kong, China.
