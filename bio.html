Yuanqiang Zhou - Biography

Yuanqiang Zhou was born in Xiangyang, Hubei. He received the B.S. degree (major: mathematics and applied mathematics) in Science in 2013 and the M.S. degree (major: guidance, control and simulation of aircraft) in control science and engineering in 2015, from the Harbin Institute of Technology, Harbin, Heilongjiang, China. Since September 2015, he has been working toward the Ph.D. degree in control science and engineering with the Department of Automation, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Shanghai, China.

From October 2017 to September 2019, he was awarded a grant from the China Scholarship Council to study as a visiting researcher for two years with the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Tandon School of Engineering, New York University, Brooklyn, NY, USA.
